  • Lending Area:

    Massachusetts – All

  • Loan Amount:

    $50,000 to $5,000,000

  • Loan Term:

    6 – 36 months

  • Interest Rates:

    9%-14% per annum*

  • Amortization:

    Interest Only

  • Max Loan to Value:

    Property Acquisitions – Up to 70% (using the as-is, after repaired, or after completed value).

    Cash-out Refinances – Up to 60%

  • Origination Fee:

    2%-6% of the total loan amount, due and payable at closing

  • Prepayment:

    No Prepayments Penalties

  • Upfront Fees:

    Beware of lenders charging upfront fees! Mass Hard Money Lender charge zero upfront fees other than a nominal appraisal fee. Once a formal commitment letter is executed, only then will we require a due diligence fee. We will discuss all fees with you upfront, without obligation.

  • Information / Documentation Needed:

    Typically an inspection/appraisal and title report

  • Mortgage Position:

    1st lien position only

  • Broker Fee:

    Absolutely no broker fees!

  • Timeframe to close:

    Once we have all the necessary information, funds can be dispersed to you in as little as 48 hours.
